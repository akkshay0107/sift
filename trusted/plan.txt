Day 1: Classic Paris + Landmarks

Morning

Start at Eiffel Tower (go early to avoid crowds)
Walk along the Seine River
Stop for coffee + pastry at a nearby café

Afternoon

Visit Arc de Triomphe
Walk down Champs-Élysées
Lunch at a casual brasserie

Evening

Seine river cruise (great way to see landmarks quickly)
Dinner near Saint-Germain
Day 2: Art + History

Morning

Explore Louvre Museum (book tickets in advance)
Focus on key pieces like the Mona Lisa

Afternoon

Walk to Notre-Dame Cathedral (exterior view if interior restricted)
Wander the Latin Quarter
Grab crepes or a quick lunch

Evening

Visit Montmartre
See Sacré-Cœur Basilica at sunset
Dinner in Montmartre (more relaxed, local vibe)
Day 3: Culture + Relaxed Exploring

Morning

Visit Musée d'Orsay (Impressionist art)
Walk through Tuileries Garden

Afternoon

Explore Le Marais (shops, food, historic streets)
Optional: visit Palace of Versailles (half/full-day trip if you prefer)

Evening

Final dinner + night walk by the Eiffel Tower (it sparkles every hour after dark)
Quick Tips
Get a metro pass (fast and cheap)
Book major attractions ahead of time
Eat slightly off main tourist streets for better food + prices
Keep some time unplanned—Paris is best when you wander a bit
